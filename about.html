About LoopWorker

The signals are already there.

Search behavior, competitor moves, customer language, pricing pressure, proof gaps, channel drift. Most businesses don't listen. LoopWorker exists to make market movement visible — before execution.

AL

I built LoopWorker because most strategy work happens before the evidence shows up. Founders ship positioning, pricing, and campaigns built on the loudest opinion in the room — and three months later, rebuild.

The signals to make the call honestly are public. Competitor ads, review fragments, search demand, pricing pages, customer language. Pulling them takes 4-12 hours per signal done right. Most teams don't have that time. So they guess.

LoopWorker is the pull. Founder-led. No team of analysts. Two active Atlas engagements at a time so the work stays mine.

How we work

Three principles. No deviation.

01

Start with the decision

Pricing, positioning, proof, channel, launch, offer, visual direction. Every Sprint is scoped around a real decision on your desk — not a research wishlist.

02

Pull live public signals

Competitors, search demand, customer voice, pricing pages, ads, content cadence, review language, proof gaps. Public data only. No surveys, no expert calls.

03

Make the next move visible

The output is not a mood board or a theory deck. It's an evidence-backed recommendation with a ranked, dated action sheet. PDF + editable doc.
