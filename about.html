Approach

The job is not to have more opinions. The job is to see the category clearly.

Start with the decision

Pricing, positioning, proof, channel, launch, offer, visual direction. The Sprint is scoped around the decision that needs evidence.

Pull live signals

Competitors, search demand, customer voice, pricing pages, ads, content cadence, review language, and proof gaps are mapped into one view.

Make the next move visible

The output is not a mood board or a theory deck. It is an evidence-backed recommendation with a ranked action sheet.

Best Fit

Who usually benefits most

  • Founders and operators with a real decision to make and a market already leaving clues.
  • Teams preparing a launch, repositioning, pricing move, channel shift, or proof reset.
  • Businesses that want clarity before spending more on execution.
Why Sprint First

The Sprint keeps execution honest.

  • It separates what the market is showing from what the room prefers.
  • It gives downstream work a source of truth.
  • It makes recommendations defendable under follow-up questions.
