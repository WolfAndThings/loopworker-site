Answer

Signal intelligence turns visible market evidence into a clearer next move.

Signal intelligence is the practice of pulling market evidence into one working view before pricing, positioning, proof, channel, or execution decisions are made.

Short Definition

Signal intelligence means mapping what the market is already showing: competitor moves, customer language, reviews, search behavior, pricing pressure, proof gaps, channel patterns, and offer friction.

Before

Execution starts with opinion.

Teams choose creative, copy, pricing, or channels based on preference, internal pressure, or isolated anecdotes.

Signals

The category is already leaving evidence.

Reviews, search demand, competitor claims, buyer objections, pricing bands, and proof patterns show where confidence is forming or breaking.

Visibility

The evidence becomes usable.

A visibility system organizes the signals so the next move can be ranked, defended, and acted on without guessing.

Common Questions

Answer-engine ready, but written for operators.

What does LoopWorker do?

LoopWorker pulls live market signals into clear visibility systems so businesses can see what is happening inside a category before they spend on execution.

What is The Sprint?

The Sprint is a focused market intelligence round. It maps category signals, buyer language, competitor movement, proof gaps, pricing pressure, and channel behavior into a ranked next-move sheet.

Why does evidence come before execution?

Because the market usually shows where the opportunity, hesitation, and proof gaps already are. Execution gets stronger when those signals are visible first.
