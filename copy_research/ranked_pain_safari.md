## ⚠️ WALLET RULE (decides which framing ships)
Public Safari sources the LOUD pain, which comes from BROKE pre-launch founders. Funded/$1M+ buyers do NOT vent publicly (they guard image). So:
- Public ponds = **vocabulary**. Past payers + sales calls = **truth**.
- **Funded buyers are repelled by survival/failure framing** ("you launched to crickets" reads as: you failed, beneath me).
- For money, reframe to **PLATEAU / ceiling**: established, has results, outgrown how they look.
- Survival framing only for sub-floor / Surface-tier traffic. NEVER lead a funded prospect with it.
