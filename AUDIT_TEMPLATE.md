# LoopWorker Content Audit Template

This is the reusable base for creating client audits. When someone submits the form, use their info to fill in each section. Scrape their Instagram, analyze their content, and deliver this.

---

## How to Use This Template

1. Client submits audit form (name, email, business, Instagram/website, business type, challenge)
2. Scrape their Instagram (last 20-30 posts) using Playwright
3. Analyze their content using the framework below
4. Fill in each section with their specific data
5. Export as a clean HTML page (like kirahri-strategy)
6. Send to their email

### By Business Type — Hook Formulas:

**Restaurant:**
- "This [dish] has been selling out by [time] every night this week."
- "The thing nobody orders but should."
- "Behind the kitchen at [time] on a [day]."
- "We almost took this off the menu. Then this happened."

**Med Spa:**
- "Most clients wait too long before starting [treatment]."
- "The question I get asked most about [treatment]."
- "What [treatment] actually looks like (no filter)."
- "The biggest mistake people make with [treatment]."

**Gym / Fitness:**
- "If you've been saying 'I'll start next week'..."
- "What your first day actually looks like."
- "Nobody talks about this part of [activity]."
- "The class that scares people the most (and why they come back)."

**Salon / Barbershop:**
- "The transformation that made me stop mid-cut."
- "This is the most requested [service] right now."
- "What [service] actually costs (and why)."
- "Before vs. after — but pay attention to [specific detail]."

**Coffee Shop:**
- "The drink nobody knows about."
- "What [time] looks like here."
- "We tested [x] and this one won."
- "The real reason people come here (it's not just the coffee)."

**General Local Business:**
- "The thing we do that nobody else does."
- "What a day here actually looks like."
- "The question I get asked the most."
- "We almost didn't [do this thing]. Here's what happened."

## ICP — IDEAL CLIENT PROFILE

### Who This Works Best For:

**Business type:** Local service businesses (restaurants, med spas, salons, gyms, coffee shops, contractors, dental, real estate)

**Revenue:** $200K-$5M/year (big enough to care about marketing, small enough to not have an agency)

**Social presence:** Has an Instagram, posts at least occasionally, but not seeing results

**Pain signals (from form):**
- "Getting views but no customers"
- "Don't know what to post"
- "Posting but nothing happens"
- "Spending too much time on content"
- "Competitors are doing better"

**Red flags (probably not a fit):**
- No Instagram at all (too early stage)
- E-commerce only (different playbook)
- "Just want more followers" (vanity metrics, not customers)

**Best fit:** A local business owner who posts 2-5x/week, gets some engagement, but can't connect it to actual revenue. They know content matters but don't know what's wrong.
