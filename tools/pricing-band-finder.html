How the band is calculated.

Three multipliers stacked on a category base price:

  • Category base. SaaS, DTC, services, creators, and local services run on different absolute price floors. SaaS modal in early stage is roughly $99/mo; services modal is roughly $3,000/project.
  • Stage multiplier. Pre-launch 0.7×, Early 1.0×, Growth 1.4×, Scale 2.0×. Larger companies anchor higher because their buyer expects depth + support.
  • Differentiation multiplier. Commodity 0.7×, Clear 1.0×, Category-defining 1.8×. Stronger positioning earns price.
