Quick questions before booking

How long is the call?

Usually 30 minutes. The purpose is fit and signal clarity: what decision matters, what evidence would be useful, and whether a Sprint is the right next step.

Do I need to prepare anything?

Bring the decision you are trying to make, plus your website, channel, or category context. One sentence is enough if the question is clear.

What happens after the call?

If The Sprint fits, I will send the smallest useful scope within 24 hours. If it does not, you will know which signal to inspect first.

What does it cost to work together?

The Sprint is scoped around the decision and the evidence required. Downstream visual, content, website, or channel execution is only discussed after visibility is clear.

What if I only need a quick read?

Use the form above and keep the decision narrow. If it can be answered without a full Sprint, we will say so.
