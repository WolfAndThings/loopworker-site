FAQ

Questions that usually come up before someone starts.

The fastest path is still the signal review, but these are the answers that tend to matter first.

How fast do I get the signal read?

Usually within 24 to 48 hours. The read focuses on the visible market signals around the decision you are trying to make.

What do you need from me?

Your name, email, business name, and the public surface you want reviewed: website, category, Instagram, offer, competitor set, or launch question.

Do you only give strategy, or do you execute too?

The Sprint is the starting point. Downstream execution only happens after the market signals are clear.

What kinds of businesses are the best fit?

Founder-led businesses, category owners, operators, and teams facing a decision where market evidence matters more than another opinion.

What if we already have content, analytics, or customer data?

That usually helps. Existing surfaces give the Sprint more signal to compare against the market.

What if I do not know which Sprint fits?

Start with the signal review. The goal is to choose the smallest Sprint that can answer the decision well.

Can you work with an internal team?

Yes. The work can support founders, operators, in-house teams, or partner teams that need clearer evidence before execution.

Do you guarantee a result?

No. Market movement still depends on the offer, execution, timing, and the business behind it. The goal is to reduce guessing before decisions are made.

Next Step

If the questions are answered, the signal review is the cleanest next move.

Bring the decision. We will look for the signals around it.
