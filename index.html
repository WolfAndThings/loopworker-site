Before you decide

Common objections.

Why not just hire a consultant?

Consultants give you opinions. Sprints give you signals — public data, ranked patterns, evidence behind every line. The PDF stands on its own.

Why not do this research in-house?

You can. The Field Manual walks through the seven core signals. Most founders try, realize each signal takes 4-12 hours done right, and decide $8,000 buys 10 days of their team's time back.

What if my market is too small for this?

If your category has at least 3 competitors, 50+ public reviews, and any search demand, the signals are there. Niche markets often produce sharper briefs because the noise floor is lower.

What's the difference between you and a research agency?

Agencies deliver decks. Sprints deliver decisions. Agencies need 6-12 weeks. Sprints take 48h to 14d. Agencies present findings. Sprints recommend moves.

What if the brief doesn't help?

14-day refund. If you can't act on a single line, write back. Happened once in two years.
