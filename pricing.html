The four Reads

Sold by the question, not the tier.

Every Read wears one of four shapes. Bring the question; the shape follows.

► The Threat Read

“This competitor is moving. How real?”

Your rival's playbook, reconstructed: ads, hiring, pricing, and timing. What to copy, what to counter, what to ignore.

► The Entry Read

“Should I launch, enter, or open?”

Who holds the ground, what buyers already ask for, whether the gap is real. Ranked entry paths, or the reasons to stay out.

► The Pricing Read

“If I move price, what happens?”

Competitor price ladders, buyer tradeoffs, and demand signals, read into ranked price moves and the risk each one carries.

► The Growth Read

“Where does my next dollar and hour go?”

Where rivals actually spend, what returns, and which channel sits unguarded. A ranked plan for the next dollar.

Whichever Read fits, the spine is the same: every material claim graded and sourced.

Pricing FAQ

Asked first.

Is this an audit?

No. An audit grades what you already have. A Read pulls the public evidence around one decision and turns it into a ranked answer.

What do I actually get?

A written Read: the recommendation on top, the evidence under it, every material claim graded high, medium, or low confidence, sources shown, risks and next moves. Everything from the 5-day Read up includes a review call.

Which depth should I choose?

Don't. Send the question and I recommend the smallest depth that answers it.

Can this use sensitive data?

Engagements are private by default. Public case studies are anonymized or only published with permission.

Refund policy?

If the Read doesn't improve the evidence for your decision, tell me within 14 days and I refund the fee. It doesn't promise a particular outcome.

Who is this NOT for?

Companies under roughly $1M in revenue without funding, decisions with less than $25K at stake, and buyers looking for commodity research.

Why is the 10-day Read $8,000 when consultants charge $50K+?

No partners, no pyramid, no 80-slide theater. Founder-led research and a Read built to be acted on rather than presented. The cost comparison page shows the full market; I sit where I sit on purpose.
