Pricing FAQ

Common questions.

Is this an audit?

No. An audit grades what you already have. The Sprint pulls public signals from the market and turns them into a next move.

What do I actually get?

One decision brief with the relevant evidence, the recommendation, and a ranked action sheet. PDF plus editable doc. Atlas + Operating System include a 30-min walkthrough call on delivery.

Which tier should I choose?

Surface to test the method. Compass for one narrow question. Atlas for the standard connected market brief — most clients land here. Operating System for a launch, repositioning, pitch-deck, or pricing architecture rewrite where 3 months of execution support actually matters.

Can this use sensitive data?

Yes. Default engagements are private. Public case studies are anonymized or only published with permission.

Refund policy?

14-day refund. If the brief lands and you cannot act on a single line, write back. Happened once in two years.
