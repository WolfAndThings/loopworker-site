Pricing FAQ

Is this an audit?

No. An audit grades what you already have. The Sprint pulls public signals from the market and turns them into a next move.

What do I actually get?

One decision brief with the relevant evidence, the recommendation, and a ranked action sheet. PDF plus editable doc.

Which tier should I choose?

Lite for one narrow question. Pro for the standard connected market brief. Full for a launch, repositioning, pitch-deck, pricing architecture, or other higher-stakes move.

Can this use sensitive data?

Yes. Default engagements are private. Public case studies are anonymized or only published with permission.

Refund policy?

If the brief lands and you cannot act on a single line, write back within 14 days for a full refund.
