  • Logo + identity design. Bring your existing logo. We work with what you have.

Pricing FAQ

Why is this cheaper than a real photographer?

Production pipeline + AI = 80% less time. Same art direction quality. Different cost structure. We're not undercutting — we're rebuilt from the ground up.

Is the Pay-in-2 a payment plan or a deposit?

Half upfront kicks off the project. Half on delivery. No interest. No financing partner. Bank transfer or Stripe.

Can I upgrade mid-project?

Yes. Pay the difference, we extend scope. Most common: Starter → Growth at the Day 7 checkpoint.

What if I'm not happy with the work?

The Day 7 checkpoint is the protection. After 25 images, you give feedback or call it. If you call it, you pay 50% and we part ways. Has not happened yet, but the safety net exists.

Do you do month-to-month retainers without a build?

Not currently. The build creates the brand DNA the retainer runs against. Without it, retainer work is generic.

Refund policy?

50% refund up to Day 7 if the direction misses. Zero refunds after Day 7 (production has happened).

Still deciding?

Book the discovery call. We'll talk fit before we talk price. Worst case, you leave with a stronger plan.
