Pricing FAQ

Common questions.

Is this an audit?

No. An audit grades what you already have. The Sprint pulls public signals from the market and turns them into a next move.

What do I actually get?

A Market Read, 30-day growth plan, campaign kit, and tracking board. PDF plus editable doc. Atlas + Market Operating System include a 30-min walkthrough call on delivery.

Which tier should I choose?

Signal Snapshot to test the method. Competitive Intelligence Brief for one narrow question. Atlas for the full Market Read and growth plan, most clients land here. Market Operating System for a launch, repositioning, pitch-deck, or pricing architecture rewrite where 3 months of execution support actually matters.

Can this use sensitive data?

Yes. Default engagements are private. Public case studies are anonymized or only published with permission.

Refund policy?

If the Market Read and growth plan do not give you a defensible move, write back within 14 days. We either correct the miss or refund it.

Who is this NOT for?

Companies under roughly $1M in revenue without funding, decisions with less than $25K at stake, and buyers looking for commodity research. Signal Snapshot is for narrow questions; Atlas and Market Operating System are for decisions where being wrong is meaningfully expensive.

Why is Atlas $8,000 when consultants charge $50K+?

No partners, no pyramid, no 80-slide theater. Founder-led research, a small operator team, and a Market Read designed to be acted on rather than presented. The cost comparison page shows the full market; we sit where we sit on purpose.
