00 - Snapshot

The affiliate play is not volume first. It is proof first.

Videfy should use affiliates to create visible launch proof, repeatable tutorial content, and a small base of revenue-linked creators. The near-term goal is not hundreds of partners. The near-term goal is a tight creator bench that can show Videfy in real workflows.

01 - What others are doing

The market already trains creators to expect recurring upside plus access.

The affiliate benchmark is not one number. AI video and creator-tool programs use a mix of recurring commission, free access, credits, co-marketing, public recognition, and selective ambassador programs. Videfy's offer should feel competitive on cash while standing out on product access and creator usefulness.

02 - How we arrived here

Videfy should sit between aggressive commission and selective creator access.

The benchmark points to one clear conclusion: a generic affiliate link is not enough for AI video creators. They need a product they can demonstrate, a reason to post now, and a commission structure worth building content around.

Why not lower than 30%
Recruiting reality
Descript and Synthesia show that 15-25% can work for established brands. Videfy is pre-launch and needs creators to take a bigger early bet, so 30% is the floor that feels worth attention.
Why not just 50%
Margin and seriousness
Pictory and InVideo show high commissions can recruit attention, but Videfy also has AI generation costs. A 30% x 12-month structure is strong without teaching affiliates to expect unsustainable economics.
Why add Pro + credits
Content creation need
Runway, Pictory, and ambassador programs show that free access is part of the pitch. Creators cannot sell Videfy well unless they can make real examples first.
Recommendation: Keep the public affiliate offer simple: 30% for 12 months, free Pro, free Generate credits. Use the 18-month extension, leaderboard, launch features, and custom demo assets as activation levers for the creators most likely to post.
03 - Affiliate offer

The offer needs to feel like access, revenue, and content.

Core offer
30% commission for 12 months, plus free Pro access and Generate credits to test the product.
Creator incentive stack
What makes the ask easier to accept
  • Free Pro account active before launch.
  • Free Generate credits so affiliates can make real demos.
  • 30% x 12 months on referred customers.
  • Top 5 monthly affiliates extended to 18 months.
  • Public leaderboard for competitive creators.
  • Launch feature opportunities for visual or tutorial creators.
Positioning note: Lead with early access and useful content, not "be our affiliate." The commission is the upside after the creator already sees an angle they can post.
04 - Creator tiers

Five tiers, five different jobs.

Tier 1 - Mid-large
50K+ followers
Aspirational creators and AI media voices. Hardest to land, but one or two wins can unlock credibility and traffic.
Tier 2 - Mid-size
5K-50K followers
Highest practical hit rate. These creators want growth, new content angles, and revenue upside.
Tier 3 - Small/on-niche
1K-5K followers
Easiest activation. Best group for fast affiliate ramp, tutorials, product feedback, and early community energy.
Tier 4 - Adjacent niches
Design, architecture, film
Not always direct-conversion creators. Useful for visual proof, aesthetic credibility, and showcase content.
Tier 5 - Borrow pedigree
Advisors, press, podcasts
Not true affiliates. Ask for feedback, quotes, mentions, podcast appearances, or advisor-level credibility.
Priority order
Launch sequence
Warm Tier 1 and Tier 5 early. Convert Tier 2 and Tier 3 fastest. Use Tier 4 selectively when the visual angle is strong.

05 - Outreach scripts

Keep the first ask small.

The scripts should be personalized before sending. The structure stays consistent: reference their work, explain the early-access angle, give the offer, and ask if they want a short demo or access.

Tier 1 script

Hey [name] - really respect your work on [specific recent post / video]. We're launching Videfy, an AI video production platform built around Claude MCP. Founders are giving 50 creators free Pro accounts + 30% x 12mo commission on referrals. Want to send you a 5-min demo + early access? No strings.

Tier 2 script

Hey [name] - love your [specific content type]. We're launching Videfy, a production platform built on Claude MCP. Already creating content matching your style and can send one example below. Free Pro + 30% x 12mo on any signups. Want early access?

Tier 3 script

Hey [name] - saw your [post]. Think you'd love what we're building. Videfy launches in [X weeks] and we're giving 100 creators free Pro accounts to test. 30% x 12mo on any signups you bring. Worth a 5-min look?

Tier 4 script

Hey [name] - your [aesthetic] is exactly the kind of work Videfy was built for. Want to feature your work on launch + give you access? Free Pro + featured spot on our home page rotation.

Tier 5 script

Hey [name] - would love to send you Videfy + a custom hero film for your audience. No affiliate ask. Just want to put it in your hands and hear what you think.

06 - Outreach cadence

Run it as an eight-week pre-launch pipeline.

Week -8

Personalized DMs to Tier 1 top 7 + Tier 5. Slow-burn warm-up.

Week -7

DM Tier 2 top 13 with a custom example reel or demo angle.

Week -6

DM Tier 3 creators with affiliate offer, Pro access, and clear content angle.

Week -5

Follow up with non-responders from Tier 1 and Tier 2.

Week -4

DM Tier 4 creators with the feature/showcase offer.

Week -3

Onboard committed affiliates. Activate Pro accounts and share content templates.

Week -2

Final follow-up wave. Lock five partner creators for Day 0 content.

Week -1

Confirm Day 0 partner creator content is scheduled or live by 10 AM ET.

08 - Tracking system

Track affiliates like a revenue channel, not a loose DM list.

Airtable fields
Minimum operating layer
  • Creator handle
  • Platform
  • Tier
  • Follower count
  • Outreach status: cold, warm, committed, active, lapsed
  • Affiliate link or code
  • Content posted count
  • Signups generated
  • Paid conversions
  • Last activity date
Weekly review
Keep the program clean
  • Review active affiliates every week.
  • Follow up with warm leads that have not posted.
  • Drop or pause affiliates inactive for 30+ days.
  • Move top performers into extended commission window.
  • Turn best creator posts into reusable proof assets.
  • Refresh the target list from current AI video hashtags.
