[PAUSED] Day +1 — 5 Places Creative Stalls
Owner: Alex Designer Editor Copywriter Scheduler Status: IDEA
copy design review schedule post
Hook
Most video ideas do not die from being bad — they stall at 1 of 5 points
Post copy / script / caption
Most video ideas do not die from being bad.

They stall at one of five points.

1. The brief is too vague to act on.
2. The visual direction is unclear, so the editor guesses.
3. The draft takes longer than the idea is exciting.
4. Feedback comes in scattered, late, and contradictory.
5. The asset gets exported but never has a clear job to do.

Map your last stuck project against these five. The bottleneck is one of them — not all.

Fix one. Watch the rest move.
