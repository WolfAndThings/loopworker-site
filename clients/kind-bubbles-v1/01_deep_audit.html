01 · The Read

You're shipping product photography in a market that pays for parental relief.

Pop-up bubble events in 2026 are not bought on aesthetic. They're bought on a parent's quiet hope: that someone has thought about this so I don't have to. Your audience is decision-fatigued — they have already bought four birthday parties this year, all of them mediocre. They are scrolling at 11pm with one thumb. The post that books them is the post that says I see you, and I've handled it.

Right now @kind.bubbles is doing something most local services do — showing the bubble. The flash, the kid, the wand. It's pretty. It performs okay. But it's interchangeable with three other bubble vendors in the Bay. The algorithm reads "interchangeable" as "ignorable" and routes the post to a smaller audience.

The fix isn't producing more content. It's producing different content, in a known ratio, with a known opening line. That's what this audit gives you.

"You don't have a content problem. You have a thumbnail-and-first-line problem. Everything past that is already working harder than you think."— The TL;DR

05 · Hook Archetypes

What hook types you're using — and which ones you've never tried.

Across the 30, here's the distribution. You're heavy on one archetype, missing four others entirely.

In Heavy Use · The "Vibe" Open

Caption opens with a feeling word ("magical", "best", "vibes"). 11 of 30 posts. This is the lowest-converting archetype on Instagram.

e.g. "Magical day", "Saturday vibes", "Best part of every event"
In Use · The Recap Open

Caption opens with location or name. 6 of 30. Useful for SEO; useless for cold scroll. Algorithm treats as low-novelty.

e.g. "Walnut Creek event last weekend", "Birthday recap — [name] turns 6"
In Use · The Question Open

Caption opens with a question. 4 of 30. Highest performer in your feed. Underused.

e.g. "Did you know bubbles are three layers?", "How does a giant bubble hold its shape?"
Rarely Used · The Counter-Position

Caption takes a stance. 1 of 30. Triggers comments & saves.

e.g. "Why we don't do face paint or balloons"
Missing · The First-Time Frame

"The first time my kid saw ___…" Zero uses. This is the parent-resonance frame — drops a parent into a memory before they've finished reading.

Add 3/month minimum.
Missing · The Insider-Truth Frame

"The thing about bubbles no one says…" Zero uses. Founder authority lane — reads as wisdom, not pitch.

Pairs perfectly with founder-on-camera reels.
Missing · The Stat / Numeric Frame

"73% of birthday parties this year had a screen running." Zero uses. Numbers in slot 1 of a reel = save magnet.

Even self-collected numbers work ("In 200 events, here's what we've learned…")
Missing · The Confession Frame

"I almost didn't start Kind Bubbles." Zero uses. Founder vulnerability is the trust accelerant. Use 1x/quarter, max — but use it.

Save for Founder Story pillar.
06 · Format Mix

The current ratio — and the one we want.

Reels carry the algorithm in 2026. Stills are for proof. Carousels are for saves. Your current ratio is reel-light and still-heavy, and the still posts are doing the carousel's job badly.

Reels Now14 of 30 · 47% Target9 of 14/mo · ~64%
Carousels Now7 of 30 · 23% Target3 of 14/mo · ~22%
Stills Now9 of 30 · 30% Target2 of 14/mo · ~14%

The shift: cut single-image stills nearly in half (most of them are doing what a carousel slide-1 should be doing), and reroute that volume into reels — specifically, the science + founder-story reel templates this audit identifies as winning.

08 · Caption Open-Line Audit

The first six words decide the post.

Across the 30, the opening line breaks into five patterns. Three of them work. Two of them don't.

Pattern 01 · Works

The Direct Question

"Did you know…", "How does…", "What if…" — opens an open loop. Feed is starved for this; you have 4 examples in 30. Aim for 4 per 14.

Pattern 02 · Works

The Identity Stack

"Single dad. Biochemist. Now this." — three nouns, three periods, no adjectives. Most-saved format on the founder lane. Reusable structure.

Pattern 03 · Works

The Counter-Position

"Why we don't…" — implicit takedown of category norm. Comments magnet. One per 14 minimum.

Pattern 04 · Buried

The Vibe Word

"Magical", "best", "perfect", "vibes". 11 of 30 posts. Zero new information for a scrolling parent. Move all to ban list.

Pattern 05 · Buried

The Recap Lead

"What a day at…", "Recap from…". Useful inside the caption (SEO), fatal as the opening line.

"Banned-from-now-on opening words: magical, perfect, beautiful, stunning, amazing, best, vibes. Replace each with a noun, a number, or a question."— The Open-Line Rule
09 · Save / Share / DM Signals

What the algorithm reads as parental trust.

Reach is decided by likes. Saves and shares decide whether a post is reshown to the same person's friends. DMs decide whether the algorithm re-prioritizes the entire account. The breakdown:

Saves

What gets saved on @kind.bubbles right now

The two carousels with parenting tips ("5 reasons sensory play matters", "Sensory play activities for rainy days"). That's it. Save-bait should be 1 of every 5 posts — currently it's 1 of every 15.

Shares

What gets shared

Visual-surprise reels (freezing bubble, 3-bubbles-inside-a-bubble). Parents tag friends. Share-bait should always pair with a tag-prompt in caption ("send this to a parent who needs to see it").

DMs

What triggers DMs

Founder Story posts. Every comment cluster on the page is on a founder post. Convert that signal: when a Founder Story performs, run a Story 24h later asking "who's planning a party right now? DM me — I want to hear about your kid." That single move = bookings.

10 · The 5 Highest-Leverage Moves

If you do nothing else for 30 days, do these five.

  1. Double Founder Story to 25% of the feed. Convert the carousel "Why I started Kind Bubbles" into a 60-second face-to-camera reel. Single highest-ROI move on the page.
  2. Ban the vibe-word opening line. No more "magical", "best", "perfect", "vibes". Replace with question, number, or identity-stack open.
  3. Cut Event Magic posts to 28% of feed. Move recap content to Stories. Free 4–5 grid slots/month for science + founder.
  4. Lead every reel with a face or surprise inside 1.5 seconds. No more setup shots. The wide goes in slide 4 of a carousel, never slide 1, never frame 1.
  5. Add one save-bait carousel every 5 posts. Parent-utility content (rainy day sensory play, screen-free birthday tips, what-to-pack lists). Sells nothing directly. Sells everything indirectly.
