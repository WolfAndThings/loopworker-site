Anchor · pin this first

One reel becomes
the top of your feed.

Every other reel here is acquisition. This one is the conversion. When a parent finds Kind Bubbles for the first time, the pinned post is what they see. It needs to do three things in 30 seconds: tell them you're a real person, tell them why this exists, tell them why their kid needs it. Shoot this once, do it well, pin it forever, change only when you have a better one.

Pinned Post · Founder Voice

The dad who started Kind Bubbles
over a kitchen sink.

Shoot setup

Saturday morning. Kitchen sink at home. Gear: iPhone on tripod, eye-level. One natural-light angle from a window. No lav mic — phone audio is fine for this format. Daughter at the kitchen table in the background, drawing or eating breakfast, NOT looking at camera.

Voiceover script · 30 seconds
0:00 · Three years ago, I left my job and my marriage in the same month.
0:05 · I had a six-year-old who didn't know what to make of me.
0:09 · So we made bubbles. At this sink. For an hour. And another hour.
0:15 · I'm a biochemist. I know why bubbles work — the surfactants, the glycerin, the way light bends through the soap film.
0:21 · But that's not what happened in our kitchen.
0:25 · What happened is, my daughter and I figured out how to be friends again.
0:30 · That's what Kind Bubbles is. It's that.

Production tips · the things that matter

Don't optimize.
Just don't break it.

Gear (you already own)

  • Phone — iPhone 12 or newer. 240fps slow-mo for R02. Vertical 9:16 for IG/TikTok, square 1:1 for LinkedIn carousels.
  • Tripod — any phone tripod with a remote shutter. Cheap on Amazon.
  • Lav mic — RØDE SmartLav+ for any voiceover sit-down. Phone audio is fine for ambient.
  • That's it — no camera, no editor, no light kit. The brand is "biochemist single dad." Don't shoot like a brand.

What to avoid

  • Trending audio — kills the founder voice. Use your own audio + voiceover ~80% of the time.
  • Heavy edits — fast cuts and motion graphics signal "agency made this." You're not that brand. Stay one step rougher than you think you should.
  • Watermarks / logos in corner — signal "ad." Skip.
  • Caption too long — under 100 words. Hook in line 1. CTA in last line.
  • Posting daily — 2 reels/week + 1 carousel + stories is enough. More burns you out + dilutes signal.

What to repeat

  • Pin R01 (anchor) until R04 (founder kitchen) is shot — then pin R04 instead. Founder content always converts higher than promotional content.
  • Shoot the 12-photo recap at every single booking — even if you don't post all 12. Build the library.
  • Voiceover yourself — your voice is the unfair advantage. Don't hire a narrator. Don't use AI voice.
  • End every reel with the same 1-second outro — text only: "Kind Bubbles · Bay Area · link in bio." Consistency = brand recall.
