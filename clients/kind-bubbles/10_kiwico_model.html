The model · five layers

What gets built.
Layer by layer.

The Kind Bubbles network is a five-layer business. Each layer feeds the next. Each layer compounds the founder narrative.

Layer 01
Founder
Chris Steele · "Therapy Dad." Single voice across IG, TikTok, YouTube, podcast appearances, and eventually a book / course. Healing-through-play, generational-trauma-breaker, parent-coach credentials. Authority is the moat.
Layer 02
Brand IP
The Kind Bubbles experience format. Standardized show structure, signature solution, music, scripts, training manual. Trademarked. Licensed to operators. Same everywhere — Bay Area, NYC, Atlanta, Chicago, Austin.
Layer 03
Operator network
Local Kind Bubbles facilitators in every major market. Year 1: SF + 1 city. Year 2: 5 cities. Year 3: 12 cities. Operators apply, train, sign brand-standards agreement, run experiences in their market under the Kind Bubbles brand. You hire the SF Bubble Man — you don't compete with him.
Layer 04
Product line
Year-2 add-on. Subscription Bubble Kits, sensory-play boxes, "Therapy Dad" parenting tools, founder book + course. Same buyer as the experience network — but the product reaches families who can't book a live event. Lovevery + KiwiCo precedent.
Layer 05
B2B + corporate
Schools, corporates, hospitality. Districts hire the network for assemblies. Tech companies hire for wellness days. Hotels use the founder brand for signature kid-experience offerings. Highest-margin lane, smallest-effort lane once the network is live.

Operator hire process · the playbook

Each operator goes through a 4-stage process before booking events under the brand. Brand standards control quality at scale — same playbook every Goldfish Swim School new franchisee runs.

  • 1 · Application — local entertainer or educator with 2+ years experience + clean record + Bay Area training visit possible.
  • 2 · Training week — Chris hosts a 5-day immersion in SF. Show structure, scripts, solution-mixing, parent-handling, safety, brand voice.
  • 3 · Brand-standards agreement — licensed use of the Kind Bubbles name, mark, content. Royalty model TBD (15–25% of bookings to corporate is industry-standard franchise rate).
  • 4 · Local launch — operator gets a city-specific landing page on the brand site, 90-day content support from corporate, intro to local Bay-Area-tier parent creators.
