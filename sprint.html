◆ FAQ

Nine questions before you click.

Which Sprint tier is right for me?

Quick fit guide: Under 30 posts of history, or just looking to validate the system? Sprint Lite. Founder-led, growing, want the bonus decks + 30-day calendar? Sprint Pro (the one most clients pick). Pre-launch, retail-bound, or need claims compliance + pricing architecture? Sprint Full. If you're not sure, book a 15-min discovery call and we'll point you to the right lane — no upsell pressure.

What if my account is too small?

Sprint Lite + Pro need at least 30 posts of history for the audit to read clean. Below 30, the data thins out and patterns get noisy. If you're truly pre-launch with no scrape-able feed, Sprint Full is the right lane — that's positioning + buyer journey + foundation work, not audit work.

Do you scrape my social profiles directly?

Yes. We pull from your live feed, your competitors' feeds, and a few other public sources we keep proprietary. Everything we cite is public data, pulled fresh — not from a stale third-party report. Every metric in the deck has a source link.

Do I need to film founder content?

You don't need to be on camera to run the deliverables — but the audit will almost certainly tell you that founder-on-camera is your highest-leverage move. About 80% of founder-led brands get that signal from their own data. We'll show you the proof; what you do with it is your call.

Can I add a Phase 2 retainer after?

Yes — we run a 90-day execution retainer ($3–5K/mo depending on cadence) that ships against the four pillars from the audit. It's not pitched in the sprint itself; we offer it only if you ask. Plenty of clients run a sprint, execute themselves, and never need a retainer.

Do I keep all the docs/IP?

Yes — full ownership. PDFs, Notion workspace, the prompt library, the hooks. You can hand them to a VA, a freelancer, an in-house team, or your nephew. We don't recycle frameworks across clients — every doc is built from your scrape.

What if I'm not satisfied?

If the sprint isn't useful, email us within 7 days of delivery and we'll refund in full — no questions, no clawback on the docs you keep. We've done this work for years; the goal is for you to feel like you got 5x the value, not for you to feel locked in.

Why these prices — what's the catch?

No catch. The prices are set so each tier is a no-brainer at its lane. Sprint Lite is a wall-breaker for founders. Sprint Pro is the sweet spot — bonus decks + calendar without the foundation overhead. Sprint Full is for brands about to spend on paid, retail, or launch — where the cost of getting positioning wrong is 10x the price.

Can I upgrade tiers later?

Yes. If you start with Sprint Lite and want the Pro bonus decks within 30 days, you pay the $800 delta and we ship them. Same for Pro → Full ($1,900 delta). No re-scoping, no proposal cycle.
