What this is

A sprint for the moment before the big move.

Use it when the business is close enough to act, but the next move still needs evidence: what to say, where to compete, what to fix first, what to charge, or what content should exist next.

Strong fit

You have a real decision in front of you.

A launch, repositioning, paid push, pitch deck, new offer, local expansion, website rebuild, or content system that needs to be grounded in what the market is already doing.

Weak fit

You only need generic content ideas.

This is not a prompt pack or a standard audit. The work starts with a business question, then pulls enough market evidence to make the answer useful.

A few questions

Some of what people ask first.

Is this an audit?

Not in the usual sense. We use audit methods, but the output is a private decision brief built around the move your business needs to make.

Do you need access to our accounts?

Usually no. Most of the work comes from public market data. If you want analytics, conversion data, or funnel behavior included, we can use read-only access.

Is every brief the same?

No. The research shape depends on the business, the category, and the question. The cards are examples — your engagement is assembled around what you actually need answered.

What do we get?

A written strategy brief with findings, screenshots, source trails, recommendations, and next actions. If production direction is part of the sprint, you also get the content structures and creative notes needed to build from it.

Does the Sprint include content production?

The core Sprint is research and direction. Production can be included when the next useful move is turning the findings into concrete posts, visuals, hooks, or campaign assets.

Who does the work?

Founder-led. Small caseload. No junior handoff. No generic template deck.
