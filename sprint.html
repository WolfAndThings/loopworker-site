Five questions

Before you click pay.

Which Sprint tier should I pick?

Under 30 posts of history? Skip Lite — book a discovery call instead. Founder-led, growing, want hooks you can shoot tomorrow? Pro. Pre-launch, headed to retail, or in a regulated category? Full. The $4,900 tier is required for medical / supplement / FDA-supervised claims work.

How is this different from a generic agency audit?

We pull live data on your category — pricing, positioning, audience, ad creative — before writing a single line of strategy. Every claim has a source. Most agency audits are vibes-on-a-deck. This is receipts in every line.

What if my data is sensitive (NDA, regulated, pre-launch)?

NDA standard, signed before kickoff. Two of three case studies on the site are NDA-protected — visible methodology, redacted brand. Regulated categories get the Claims Guardrail layer (Pro and Full). Pre-launch is what Sprint Full was built for.

Do I keep everything? Can you reuse it?

Non-exclusive perpetual worldwide license on every deliverable. You own it. We retain the right to use anonymized methodology in case studies (with your permission). Source data files (Apify exports, scrape JSONs) are yours.

What if it's not useful?

7-day satisfaction guarantee. Read the deck, watch the Loom. If it's not useful, email me — full refund, you keep the docs. Zero clawback. Two paid clients in May, zero refunds. We bet on the work.
