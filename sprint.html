How it works

Common questions.

How much of my time does this take?

One 30-min kickoff call to frame the decision. One 30-min walkthrough call on delivery (Atlas + OS). Maybe one async DM if I need clarification. That's it.

Why no surveys or interviews?

Surveys ask people what they think they think. The market shows you what they actually do. Public signals are higher fidelity for most strategic decisions.

What if my market is too niche?

If your category has at least 3 competitors with public web surface and any search demand, the signals are there. Niche markets often produce sharper briefs because the noise floor is lower.

How much team time does this save?

One signal pulled deep across 10 competitors is 12-20 hours of senior time. Seven signals = 80-150 hours total. A Sprint compresses three to four weeks of in-house research into 21 days, hand-rolled, ranked, ready to act on.

Is this an AI tool?

No. Founder-led. I use AI as a research aid the same way I use Google. The pattern recognition + ranking + decision call is mine.

What if I need a different decision answered?

Bring it on the kickoff call. Sprints adapt. Common: pricing architecture, positioning shifts, channel selection, proof asset priority, conversion friction diagnosis, launch readiness.

Refund?

14 days. If you can't act on a single line, write back. Happened once in two years.
