A few questions

Some of what people ask first.

Which Sprint tier should I pick?

If you have one narrow question, Lite is enough. If several signals need to be connected, Pro is the stronger fit. If the decision is high-stakes — launch, fundraise, retail, repositioning, regulated category — Full is the right engagement size.

How is this different from a normal strategy brief?

We build from public market signals first: pricing, positioning, audience language, search demand, proof, content, and competitor paths. Every major claim has a source trail. The point is decision support, not a polished opinion.

What if my data is sensitive (NDA, regulated, pre-launch)?

NDA standard, signed before kickoff. Two of three case studies on the site are NDA-protected — visible methodology, redacted brand. Regulated categories get the Claims Guardrail layer (Pro and Full). Pre-launch is what Sprint Full was built for.

Do I keep everything? Can you reuse it?

Non-exclusive perpetual worldwide license on every deliverable. You own it. We retain the right to use anonymized methodology in case studies only with your permission. Raw source exports can be included when useful.

What if it's not useful?

Read the brief and watch the walkthrough. If the work is not useful, email me within 7 days. We will either correct the issue or refund the engagement. You keep the material already delivered.
