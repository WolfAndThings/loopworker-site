A few questions

Some of what people ask first.

Is this an audit?

Not in the usual sense. We use audit methods, but the output is a private market intelligence brief built around the decisions your business needs to make.

Do you need access to our accounts?

Usually no. Most of the work comes from public market data. If you want analytics, conversion data, or funnel behavior included, we can use read-only access.

Is every brief the same?

No. The research shape depends on the business, the category, and the question. The cards are examples — your engagement is assembled around what you actually need answered.

What do we get?

A written strategy brief with findings, screenshots, source trails, recommendations, and next actions. Source files included where useful.

Who does the work?

Founder-led. Small caseload. No junior handoff. No generic template deck.
