01
Review objection clusters

Which trust gap shows up most: pricing surprise, pain, downtime, provider rotation, results variance, or upsell pressure.

Lighting consistency, angle reuse, watermarking patterns. Buyers can smell stock photos. So can Google.

Who names the starting price, who hides it, what the category-wide expectation has shifted to in your city.

Photos, hours accuracy, Q&A active, review reply rate. Local pack ranking moves on these signals.

"Is it safe / does it hurt / how long is recovery". the questions buyers Google before consult. Your FAQ should answer them in plain language.
