Pricing patterns across all categories.

The anchor-to-modal ratio holds across SaaS at ~3-4x. The modal-to-floor ratio runs ~3-5x. Categories with stronger differentiation (CRM, marketing automation, vertical SaaS) push anchor higher; categories near commodity (productivity, collab) compress ratios.

Stage matters more than category for the absolute price band. Early-stage anchors run 50-70% of late-stage anchors in the same category. The modal tier moves 20-30% over the same lifecycle.

How the data was assembled.

Named public sources

  • Public pricing pages: 60+ companies' public price pages scraped + categorized as of May 2026. Notable: Salesforce, HubSpot, Monday, Asana, Atlassian, Notion, Zendesk, Intercom, Toast, Mindbody, Clio, Procore.
  • OpenView Partners: 2024 SaaS Benchmarks report + Expansion SaaS Benchmarks. openviewpartners.com/blog
  • ChartMogul: 2024 SaaS Index + ARR + ACV benchmarks. chartmogul.com/blog
  • Bessemer Venture Partners: State of the Cloud 2024 + vertical SaaS analyses. bvp.com/atlas
  • Tomasz Tunguz: 5-year pricing research archive + tier-shape studies. tomtunguz.com

Ranges shown are floor (10th-30th percentile), modal (40th-60th), anchor (80th-95th) across publicly disclosed pricing within each category. Where pricing pages obscure full ranges with "talk to sales" gates, the implied anchor was inferred from analyst-reported deals and excluded from the floor/modal columns.
