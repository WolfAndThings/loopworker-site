◆ FAQ

Eight questions before you click.

What if my account is too small?

If you have at least 30 posts, the audit works. Below 30, the data thins out and the patterns get noisy. We've audited 200-follower accounts and 50K-follower accounts with the same system — what matters is post history, not vanity metrics.

Do you scrape my social profiles directly?

Yes. We pull from your live feed, your competitors' feeds, and a few other public sources we keep proprietary. Everything we cite is public data, pulled fresh — not from a stale third-party report. Every metric in the deck has a source link.

What if I don't post much yet?

If you have 30+ posts of any platform history, you're fine. If you're truly pre-launch with no scrape-able feed, the better fit is the foundation sprint (separate engagement) — that's positioning + buyer journey work, not audit work. Email us if you're not sure which lane you're in.

Do I need to film founder content?

You don't need to be on camera to run the deliverables — but the audit will almost certainly tell you that founder-on-camera is your highest-leverage move. About 80% of founder-led brands get that signal from their own data. We'll show you the proof; what you do with it is your call.

Can I add a Phase 2 retainer after?

Yes — we run a 90-day execution retainer ($3–5K/mo depending on cadence) that ships against the four pillars from the audit. It's not pitched in the audit itself; we offer it only if you ask. Plenty of clients run the audit, execute themselves, and never need a retainer.

Do I keep all the docs/IP?

Yes — full ownership. PDFs, Notion workspace, the prompt library, the hooks. You can hand them to a VA, a freelancer, an in-house team, or your nephew. We don't recycle frameworks across clients — every doc is built from your scrape.

What if I'm not satisfied?

If the audit isn't useful, email us within 7 days of delivery and we'll refund in full — no questions, no clawback on the docs you keep. We've done this work for years; the goal is for you to feel like you got 5x the value, not for you to feel locked in.

Why $700 — what's the catch?

No catch. The price is set so it's a no-brainer for a founder hitting a content wall — not so it's a "consulting fee." We make our money on retainers when the audit proves the gap and the client wants help executing. Most clients run the audit, execute themselves, and we never speak again. That's a feature.
