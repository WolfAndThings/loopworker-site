01 · The Read

You're not selling bubbles. You're selling a feeling — screen-free, sensory, parent-and-kid-together joy. That's the whole brand. That's why people book you over a clown or a bounce house.

Your content right now shows the product (bubbles, events, setups) but not the transformation (the look on a 4-year-old's face, a parent setting their phone down to watch). The gap between "what we do" and "what it feels like" is where every booking decision happens — and it's where your content should live.

Close that gap and you stop competing with every other kids' entertainer in the Bay Area. You become the one parents tag their friends in and screenshot for later.

03 · What We Spotted (Last ~10 Posts)

Observation 01

The bubbles are the hero — the humans aren't

Most posts frame the bubble (the product). The scroll-stopper is the reaction: kid mid-laugh, adult caught off guard, hand reaching up. The parent scrolling wants to picture their kid there — give them that.

Observation 02

Hooks are captions, not openers

First line of a caption should stop the scroll on its own. Right now the captions describe what's happening. The opening line needs to do one of three things: ask a question, flip an assumption, or name a moment ("The first time my kid saw one of these, he forgot how to talk for 6 seconds.").

Observation 03

Founder story is underused

Biochemist → teacher → single dad → bubble business. That's an unfair content advantage — no competitor has it. Even 1 reel a month in your voice (not event footage) would shift how people perceive the brand from "vendor" to "person I trust with my kid's birthday."

06 · Your Unfair Advantages

These are things nobody else in your market has. Most brands get one. You have three.

Advantage 01 — Origin

Biochemist → teacher → single dad → founder

That story sits at the intersection of trust (you get kids), credibility (you get science), and warmth (you get parents). No competitor has that stack.

Advantage 02 — Positioning

Screen-free & sensory in a screen-saturated market

Every parent in your audience feels guilty about screen time. You are the antidote. Lean into that — it's a positioning competitors can't copy by changing their feed.

Advantage 03 — Product

The moment itself is already camera-ready

Bubbles + children = inherent scroll-stop. Most businesses have to create content moments. You walk into them. You're just not capturing them with intent yet.
