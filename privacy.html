Privacy Policy

Effective March 29, 2026

This page explains what information LoopWorker collects through the website, how that information is used, and who it is shared with.

Information collected

If you submit the signal review form or contact LoopWorker directly, the site may collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your business name, website, Instagram handle, or other context you provide
  • Your notes about current content challenges or decisions
  • Basic analytics data such as page visits and referring sources

How the information is used

Information submitted through the site is used to review your request, reply to you, deliver the Sprint, and improve the website and service offer.

Third-party services

LoopWorker uses third-party services to process form submissions and site analytics, including Formspree, FormSubmit, Calendly, Plausible Analytics, and Stripe for payment processing. These services only receive the information needed to perform those functions.

Data sharing

LoopWorker does not sell your personal information. Information is only shared with service providers needed to run the site, process submissions, or respond to your request.

Data retention

Submitted information may be retained for business records, Sprint delivery, follow-up, and future project discussions unless deletion is requested.

Your choices

If you want your submitted information updated or deleted, email alex@loopworker.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to alex@loopworker.com.
