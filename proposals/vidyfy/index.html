Questions This Answers Up Front

These are the likely clarification questions. It is better to answer them in the plan than wait for the meeting to get stuck on terminology.

What does “core engine” mean?

It means the repeatable monthly system: creator education content for Instagram and TikTok, steady direct outreach, light onboarding follow-up, and a weekly review of what is actually getting replies and first posts.

What does “automated content” mean here?

It does not mean spam or fully hands-off posting. It means building reusable templates, hook structures, prompt patterns, and editing workflows so content can be produced faster and more consistently with less manual overhead.

What kind of posts are included?

These counts assume lightweight, templated short-form posts built from a repeatable system. They do not assume high-production shoots, large custom edit packages, or full campaign builds every month at these rates.

What counts as outreach?

Outreach means curated creator targeting, personalized DMs or invites, follow-ups, and the conversations needed to move someone from interest into signup or onboarding. It does not mean generic blast messaging.

Who actually publishes the content?

By default, the work assumes content is prepared in a ready-to-post state through the agreed workflow. Direct posting can be coordinated, but only if account access, approvals, and the internal handoff process are in place.

What counts as proof?

Proof means visible signs that real creators are using the platform successfully: creator spotlights, first posts, repeat activity, onboarding wins, and stories that make the next creator more likely to say yes.

What is under our control vs not?

Content output, targeting, outreach volume, templates, and follow-up structure are controllable. Creator reply rates, creator quality, and how quickly the market responds are influenced by the offer, product, and timing, so those are signals to improve rather than hard guarantees.

What does “hybrid” mean?

Hybrid means adding a human trust layer on top of faceless educational content. That can include creator spotlights, face-led explainers, trust-building videos, or more visible creator-success content once the base engine is already working.

What is not included in this plan?

This does not include paid media buying, creator incentive budget, platform ad spend, or heavy daily community moderation. It also does not assume full-time embedded marketing ownership at these rates.

What do you need from Vidyfy?

One clear point of contact, timely approvals, access to the product and creator offer, brand or product assets where needed, and support from the existing editor or designer when a post requires more than the lightweight templated workflow.
