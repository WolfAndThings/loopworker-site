Frequently answered before sign-off

What if a month is light on footage?

We have a buffer of 30+ b-roll clips from Phase 1 to fill any gap. If footage runs critically low, we trigger a "footage refresh week" — you record 60 min in one sitting, we cut from that for 6+ weeks.

What if I want to pause?

Pause for up to 60 days without canceling. Subscription resumes at the same rate. No "we already lost your slot" pressure.

Can I scale up if it's working?

Yes. After Month 3, we can move to "Pillar+" — same scope plus paid ads creative direction (+$1,500/mo) or to "Full Studio" (16-24 videos/month, +$2K/mo).

How are revisions handled?

One review pass per month included. Beyond that, $300/round. Brand DNA changes after Phase 1 sign-off = scope change, quoted separately.

Do I own the videos?

Yes. Non-exclusive perpetual worldwide license on each delivery. We retain the right to use anonymized clips in our portfolio (with permission).

What if it's not working after 30 days?

Cancel anytime — 30-day notice. Month 1 work delivered before cancellation is yours. No claw-back.
