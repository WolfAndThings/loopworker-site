The research → machine stack

  • Research phase · competitor profiles, audience platform analysis, niche winning hooks, fee benchmarks, event landscape mapping, gap identification.
  • Speaker EPK · live page + print-ready PDF on your brand. Bio in three lengths, signature talks, testimonials, press, fee transparency.
  • Speaker bureau submissions · eSpeakers, BigSpeak, Premiere, Washington Speakers, niche-specific bureaus surfaced by the research.
  • Personalized event pitch pipeline · named organizers identified by research, recommended talks per audience, emails in your voice, send tracker.
  • Award nominations · drafted + submission-ready. Industry awards research-surfaced and stack-submitted in coordinated windows.
  • Voice extraction · 35-prompt structured recording becomes the voice bank every email and post is written against.
  • Website fixes · broken pages restored, asset surfaces lifted, SEO + schema, dedicated /speaking and /press pages.
  • Content engine · LinkedIn cadence, podcast pitches, post-event amplification.

Best fit for

  • Speakers and advisors with 10+ years of practitioner work but a digital footprint that undersells.
  • Experts with a forthcoming book, recent press, or a known signature talk who want to translate that into booked stages.
  • Family-business operators, executive coaches, board governance advisors, and other category authorities whose audience is high-trust + relationship-driven.
  • People who want one person running the entire stack, not five vendors duct-taped together.
