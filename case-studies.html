Case Studies

How the content changes when it is built around action instead of filler.

These are concise examples rather than inflated success claims. The pattern is the important part: clarify the angle, build stronger hooks, and make the next step easier to say yes to.

Restaurant · My Burger

From generic menu posts to same-day visit content.

The shift was toward stronger urgency, more specific food angles, and captions that give a nearby customer a reason to come in tonight.

  • Before: generic menu visuals and passive captions
  • Shift: sellout items, stronger hooks, same-day prompts
  • Outcome: more saves, shares, and local interest signals
Service Business · Marina / Fitness

From generic posting to trust-building content.

The shift was away from empty promotional posts and toward authority, education, and first-step clarity for the buyer.

  • Before: broad offer posts with weak differentiation
  • Shift: more education, clearer fit, stronger CTA framing
  • Outcome: better DM quality and stronger inquiry intent
Property · Real Estate / Hospitality

From interchangeable content to premium perception.

The shift was visual and strategic: fewer generic room or listing shots, more atmosphere and identity.

  • Before: content that looked like every other listing
  • Shift: cinematic framing, stronger mood, clearer saves logic
  • Outcome: more memorable content and stronger interest quality
