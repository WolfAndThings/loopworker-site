01 · Top tier

McKinsey & Company

The default brand for board-level strategy. Their pedigree is the deliverable as much as the deck. Most McKinsey value is in the optics, the analyst pool, and the partner who shows up in stakeholder meetings.

Cost
$250K–$2M+
Timeline
8–16 weeks
Team size
3–8 + partner
Your time
50–100 hours
Best forF500 transformations · M&A · board-level repositioning where the McKinsey name on the deck is part of the goal.
Skip ifYou're a founder, the decision is single + tactical, you need it under 8 weeks, or you don't have stakeholder politics to manage.

Methodology

Cost ranges aggregated from public RFP responses, ex-consultant interviews, LinkedIn job descriptions, ConsultingMag salary disclosures, and operator self-reports from 2024–2026 engagements.

Timelines based on standard engagement scope per firm (phase length, not multi-phase total). Team sizes reflect typical mid-market engagement; F500 work scales up.

"Your time" estimates assume cooperative engagement (workshops, interviews, document reviews, weekly check-ins). Actual time can vary significantly.
